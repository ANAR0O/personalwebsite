Task Manager Application

  • Tech Stack: TJava, NodeJS, Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, AWS(S3, EC2, SQS)
  • Github URL: Project Link

Task Organization: The app lets you create, categorize, and prioritize tasks effortlessly. Whether it's work projects, personal chores, or important errands, you can manage everything in one place.

Intelligent Scheduling: Schedule tasks with precision. Set due dates, reminders, and recurring tasks to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Task Progress Tracking: Keep tabs on your task progress with intuitive visual indicators. See which tasks are pending, in progress, or completed at a glance.

Customizable Labels and Tags: Organize tasks with customizable labels, tags, and colors. Tailor your task management system to suit your unique preferences.

Notes and Attachments: Add notes, files, and attachments to tasks for comprehensive context, making it easier to tackle them effectively.
